  • OVERVIEW

    Due to Covid-19, people stay at home more frequently therefore the demand for using computers and the amount of time they spend on them also increases significantly. Video games have already been an integral part in today world not only during covid-19 period but also for daily entertainment demand of people. Therefore, developing a game to meet that demand is an interesting idea to make. In summary, this project focuses mainly on building up a game using tools to meet the demand during the pandemic.

    Motivation

    For the gaming industry during covid-19, especially, Nintendo corporation has drastically growth in its profit and sale in comparison to those of company before the pandemic (Mochizuki 2020). Those examples prove that entertainment industry is an attractive and potentially and limitless in developing today and the near future. Moreover, as a player, I want to create my own masterpiece that everyone can join, play and enjoy it together during leisure times. Games can be developed by various tools such as Unity, Cocos2d, etc.

    Description

    This project aims to create an arcade game such as flappy bird which was quite simple but extremely hot in 2013 as you just need to tap the screen to make the bird fly to dodge the obstacles and people in those days spent their hours and days addicted to it as it was sometimes make players annoying.

    There are various advantages while choosing to make an arcade game. Firstly, when doing this project individually, it is an affordable choice for individuals who want to work independently as it does not have to be too fussy in its graphics but primarily for a compelling content that can easily attract players. According to the Forbes (2014), Flappy bird was created in just only two days by a Vietnamese game-creator called Dong Nguyen that brought back $50,000 for him as this game is free to play due to the number of more than 50 million users throughout its heyday. Secondly, “instant” arcade games seem not to be less time consuming in comparison with online PC games and they are suitable to play during short break periods. Most of the “instant” arcade games are not too focused on building up the plot content or seem to be, the game experience will become more flexible and easier because the player will not need to spend too much time for learn about games before playing them. Moreover, such “instant” games take a very little time to experience, the maximum will be 15-20 minutes for around 5-6 attemps, compared to online games like “League of Legends” or PUBG, it usually wastes around 30 to 60 mins for 1 attempt experience only. Nevertheless, games with simple graphics will decrease the amount of data capacity that will save memory for mobiles in comparison with high graphic ones. Simple graphics games not only take less space in storing but also helps developers optimize funding for game production. Lastly, the simpler the graphics, the more appealing to players based on the trend of today's gaming market.

    This project idea is named the “FiBE” game that will be created for mobile use only. This game project is mainly based on the five basic elements which are metal, wood, water, fire & earth. The player's task is to control the character across the areas and terrain that possesses the five elements. Players can also customize their own five elements to suit the current terrain. For example, if the current terrain of the game is wood, the player must use the character of the fire attribute to get through this level. Note, the pace and difficulty of the game will increase with the level of each level in the game.

    On the other hand, there are many difficulties for this project to be done and the biggest barrier is the lack of skills and techniques to finalize my project. Due to the complexity of the game making steps, I need to learn and practice more in the next 2-3 years in order to achieve enough knowledge to make my own game. At the moment, ideas are just simply ideas.

    What is cloud computing?

    Since the beginning of the industrial revolution 4.0 period up to now, cloud computing and cybersecurity become integral parts of the development of today world. According to the Microsoft (2020), cloud computing is the most popular storage tool for over half of the century with many magnificent advantages including cost efficiency, flexibility, agility and globally scale (Microsoft 2020). There are 4 types of cloud services, for example, Infrastructure as a service (IaaS), Platform as a service (PaaS) and Software as a service (SaaS) (Stephan & Frank 2018). In fact, cloud computing has already been on its heyday from the early 1960s till now and in the future, its position will remain unchanged. Unless human might create some kind of “space computing” or “universe computing” that might threaten the cloud’s position with bigger data storage and more efficient technologies go along with them. In fact, cloud computing changes the way people store their data significantly due to the disappearance of CDs, USBs, memory disks and so on.   • CYBERSECURITY AND PRIVACY

    What is cybersecurity?

    In the industrial revolution 4.0 era, the existence of cyber security becomes the most significant solution for remaining and protecting the cloud computing and other IT aspects at the same time. There are many different kinds of cyber threats such as malware, phishing, ransomware and so on (Taylor 2020). The major issues to cybersecurity today are cybercrimes, cyber-attacks and cyber terrorism by disrupting or unlawfully infiltrating infrastructure with malicious codes to leak data or to earn income (Kaspersky 2020). Malware, which is classified as a malicious software, is the world’s most prevalent cyber hazard by damaging user’s devices. It is also camouflaged as an email attachment or a software download for making profits or political assaults (Kaspersky 2020). There are various types of malware (Taylor 2020). According to the Kaspersky (2020), cyber security, which is known as computer security or IT security, is defined as the prevention of computers, mobiles, severs, … from anonymous attacks. Cyber security includes network security, information security, application security and so on (Kaspersky 2020). The fact is that the existence of cyber security is endless as it is a guard to protect people from cyber threats. In fact, in order to improve cybersecurity, the United States spends around $ 10 billion annually, and the figure is increasingly growing year by year (Dean 2015). Network users can secure their information through (Goel 2018).

    IMPACTS

    What is the likely impact?

    The reality is that every year, cyberattacks inflict billions of US dollars in harm and directly impact every industry engaged in the use of computers and networks. The more popular cloud computing becomes, the more important the existence of cyber security is. Besides, online users care a lot about their online privacy while searching and updating their personal information onto social media. Many websites and apps track their own users’ data and sell that information. Moreover, hackers can easily hack into their personal devices by using viruses or malwares. In addition, cyber security in today world is extremely important therefore the number of cyber security jobs keep increasing. However, there are various anti-virus applications developed in order to keep the data safe such as Kaspersky, BKAV, CleanMyMac and so on. Today, approximately 25% of users use VPN as a method to protect their devices when linking to Wi-Fi hotspots, depending on the publisher's protection commitments. The reality, however, is that users have no means of checking a VPN's security, and one research has shown that most VPN publishers trick their clients (Khan & Rodriguez 2019). Lastly, about the employment, with the budget up to $ 10 billion from the US, we can see how big this industry is – cybersecurity industry. Thus, more job opportunities are created in the near future as the number of data keeps increasing days by days goes along with the demand of data protection from cyber-attacks.
